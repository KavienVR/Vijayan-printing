Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have answers.

What is the minimum order quantity?

We do not enforce minimums! You can order just 1 custom printed shirt, bag, or pillow cover to try our top quality.

Can I wash the garments normally?

Yes. All of our printed garments are machine-wash friendly. We suggest washing inside-out with cold water for optimal preservation.

What file formats do you accept?

We accept PNG, JPG, SVG, and PDF layouts. For best quality cloth prints, high-resolution formats are strongly encouraged.
